About me

A strategist who makes AI work for people

I'm Mujtaba, a former economics educator who served with the Ministry of Education in the Maldives. In 2020 I made a deliberate choice — to relocate to the UAE to be with my family and focus on what mattered most at the time.

That pause turned into a pivot. Over the past year I've been building a foundation in networking, CompTIA Security+, and AI — not just as a learner, but as someone who thinks strategically about how these tools can be put to work.

My strength isn't just technical knowledge. It's knowing how to plan, delegate, and get results — whether that's through people or AI. I come with ideas, I research relentlessly, and I build systems that work.

I'm now returning to professional life with a focus on IT, AI, and automation — and this portfolio is where that journey lives.
